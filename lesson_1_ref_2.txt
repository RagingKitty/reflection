In the long run,
1) Work can be easily put to comparison to check for bugs
2) Easily to maintain and update the app without worrying that the new code will break something.
3) Other developers in the same project can also view and provide feedbacks or changes along the way while working on the same project.
4) You could also share your codes with other programmers and when changes appear, you can check on them through the history.
5) In case of emergency, there is always a back up versino of the code which is ready to use.
